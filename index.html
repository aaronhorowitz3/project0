<projects>

Stance

Stance is a tool for tracking real-time and historical stock price data. The simple interface is designed to provide essential stock data points - value and percentage changed - without all the clutter found on most stock tracking dashboards. This was a group project, in which I worked on both the front-end and back-end. Built with Python, Django, jQuery, D3, and AJAX.

Check out Stance!

All Ways

All Ways is a web app that combines public transit (BART) directions with driving directions with one click. It is a great resource for users that drive to BART and ride the train to their final destination. All Ways was a partner project, in which I worked on both the front-end and back-end. Built with JavaScript, Axios, and Mongo/Express/Node.

Check out All Ways!

Bold Statements

Bold Statements is a forum for posting, tracking, and verifying predictions and outlandish claims made by friends, reporters, and other public figures. This was a solo project for me. Built with Python, Django, and jQuery.

Check out Bold Statements on my Github!

From Darlings to Dynasty

From Darlings to Dynasty is a story-telling site that details the Golden State Warriors' journey from lovable losers to the most dominant NBA franchise of the past decade. This was a solo project I made, utilizing only front-end languages.

Check out From Darlings to Dynasty on my Github!
